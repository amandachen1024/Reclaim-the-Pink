Frequently Asked Questions

The pink tax refers to the extra amount women pay daily as consumers. Although these products are often the same but with different packaging, the price discrepancies show a pattern of gender-based pricing.

Through using our website’s “Check” feature, we can see if your product is marked above, below, or approximately the same as other uni-sex marketed products!

By verifying your product doesn’t have gender-based pricing or purchasing products from local, women-owned businesses, you can avoid the pink tax and reclaim the pink!

By clicking the “Feature Me!” button on the home page, you can feature your local, women-owned business and provide an alternative to “pink-tax affected” products!

We are planning to implement an app for Reclaim the Pink with a product scanner, expand our locations, and feature more “pink-tax affected” products.

You can email us at hello@reclaimthepink.ca with any questions, concerns, or feedback, or use our chatbot to pass us on a message.
